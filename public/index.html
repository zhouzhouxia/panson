Every Time You Leave, a Worry Lingers

As pet owners, we all get it. When work or unexpected events take us away from home, a sense of unease creeps in. Is my pet safe? Are they eating well? Is the way I care for them truly scientific?

Safety Anxiety

Worrying about accidents happening while they're alone, without our knowledge.

Care Gap

Fearing they won't get timely care due to overtime or being away.

Scientific Care Doubts

Unsure if our feeding and exercise routines are truly best for their health.

Is there a product that ensures our pets receive **thoughtful, personalized, and informed** care, even when we're far away?
Now, there is TAIL.

TAIL, More Than Just a Collar

It's an AI-powered smart terminal that integrates monitoring, analysis, and control. Wherever you are, you can easily check on your pet and manage their home environment.

Key Selling Point 1

PetLink™ Whole-Home Linkage

TAIL is your pet's smart life manager. It seamlessly connects with smart litter boxes, feeders, cameras, and more. Even when you're not home, you can remotely manage their diet, potty breaks, and routine to create the most thoughtful environment.

Slogan: Uninterrupted care—your pet is thoughtfully looked after, even when you're not home.

Key Selling Point 2

AI-Powered Pet Care

TAIL doesn't just record data—it understands it. Through vocal and behavior analysis, it interprets your pet's emotions and needs. Combined with health data, it intelligently recommends custom meal plans and exercise routines.

Slogan: Understand their language, love their world.

Key Selling Point 3

Professional Medical Assistance

We've partnered with veterinary clinics in major cities, supporting one-tap uploads and syncing of your pet's electronic medical records. Backed by data from 500+ veterinary professionals, we safeguard your pet's health.

Slogan: One-tap upload of e-medical records, supported by 500+ veterinary professionals.

Key Selling Point 4

Comprehensive Health Monitoring

From heart rate and temperature to activity levels and sleep quality, plus GPS tracking, TAIL records your pet's vital data 24/7. Get instant status updates and timely alerts for emergencies, covering both health and safety.

Slogan: Not just a 'monitor'—a true guardian of health.

Key Selling Point 5

Long-lasting Battery

Equipped with a high-capacity battery, a single charge lasts for days. Whether it's daily commutes, long trips, or travel, TAIL stays online to update your pet's status, so you never miss a critical moment.

Slogan: Maxed-out battery, uninterrupted protection. "Always online in your pet's world."

About Us - PANSON

PANSON is a global mid-to-high-end pet electronic consumer products company, dedicated to creating a better pet-keeping experience through innovative technology. We firmly believe that the power of technology can redefine the relationship between humans and pets, making companionship smarter, more convenient, and full of fun.

The TAIL series smart collar is our core product, integrating advanced AI algorithms and whole-home smart solutions. Our vision is to become a leading brand in the global pet electronic consumer products field and to create a happier, better life for pet owners worldwide.
